Shine for Philips Hue
An app for Philips Hue lights
$2.99

Shine for Philips Hue is an app to control Philip Hue wifi-connected lightbulbs straight from your computer.

You are only one press away from turning the lights on or off. Home automation was never faster. Choose a dynamic scene, dim the lights or chose from over 16k colors for your lights — all from the comfort of your browser.

Works on Mac, Windows, Linux, and Chromebooks.

Perfect lighting, just at the right time
  • You are one click away from turning the lights on and off.
  • Dim the lights down to the right level with one mouse drag
  • Group the lights into the perfect combination, right from the app
